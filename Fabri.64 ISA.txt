Control Flow Operations:
NOP
HALT
JMP <Flag reg> <Reg2>

ALU OPCODES:
ADD <reg1> <reg2> <regOut>
SUB <reg1> <reg2> <regOut>
MUL <reg1> <reg2> <regOut>
DIV <reg1> <reg2> <regOut>
AND <reg1> <reg2> <regOut>
OR <reg1> <reg2> <regOut>
XOR <reg1> <reg2> <regOut>
NOT <reg1> <reg2> <regOut>


REGISTERS OPCODES:
REG-SET <reg1> <value>
REG-CP <reg1> <output-register>


RAM OPCODES:
RAM-SET <Addresses reg> <value reg>
RAM-GET <Addresses reg> <output-register>
